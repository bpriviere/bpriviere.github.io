About Me:
I work on the intersection of machine learning and autonomous planning, with applications in robotics and self-driving vehicles. I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Graduate Aerospace Laboratories at the California Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Soon-Jo Chung. I collaborate closely with Prof. Yisong Yue and Dr. Wolfgang Hoenig. I received the B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Stanford University in 2017, and the M.S. in Aeronautics from Caltech in 2018.
